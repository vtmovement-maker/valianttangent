PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Valiant Tangent ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website (valianttangent.com).

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of information necessary to facilitate communication through our Contact/Collaboration form. By using this form, you may optionally provide:

  • Name (Optional): To address you in our reply.
  • Email Address (Optional): Used exclusively to respond to your inquiry.
  • Message (Required): The content of your inquiry, feedback, or collaboration idea.

If you choose not to provide a Name or Email Address, your message will be submitted anonymously, and we will not be able to reply to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

If you provide your email address and name alongside a message, we use this information solely for the purpose of communicating with you regarding your specific message.

We do not:

  • Add you to any marketing newsletters or mailing lists.
  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.
  • Use your information for targeted advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

To safely process your messages without exposing our internal email addresses to spam, we utilize the following third-party services:

  • Web3Forms: We use Web3Forms to capture and forward your form submission securely to our email inbox. Web3Forms acts as a data processor. You can review their privacy policy at web3forms.com/privacy.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile: We use Cloudflare Turnstile to protect our website from spam and abuse. Turnstile verifies that you are a human without using privacy-invasive tracking cookies. You can review Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

4. Data Retention

We retain the emails we receive from you only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose of your inquiry or collaboration request. Once the communication is concluded, we may delete the correspondence from our inbox at our discretion.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location (such as under the GDPR in Europe or the CCPA in California), you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided to us via the contact form. To exercise these rights, please submit a new inquiry through the contact form detailing your request.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date".

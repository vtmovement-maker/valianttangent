About

Valiant Tangent exists in the tension between mechanical precision and human breaking points. It is a project that avoids fixed functions or predetermined paths, choosing instead to move through the space where digital grit meets organic reality.

The work is a direct reaction to the world around us. It is built on the belief that we only truly know we are here through the reflection of others. Beyond the systems and the roles, we find our reality in the shared pulse that proves we are more than just isolated observers.
